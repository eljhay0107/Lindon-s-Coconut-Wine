About Us

Lindon's Coconut Wine is a family business rooted in the heart of Brgy. Potot, where our beloved "Tuba" is crafted with generations of tradition and love. Our logo, featuring a coconut tree, a bottle of our finest Tuba, and our founder Lindon, embodies the strength and abundance of our heritage. We take pride in sharing our family's passion for quality and creating unforgettable moments with every sip.

Lindon's Coconut Wine

Tuba, also called Coconut Wine, is a special Filipino drink made from fermented coconut juice. It's refreshing, a little sweet, and has a unique earthy flavor.

RED COCONUT WINE

Red coconut wine's origins in the Philippines are unclear, likely a result of natural fermentation processes, regional variations, and evolving traditional practices.

WHITE COCONUT WINE

White Coconut Wine is a pure, unfiltered, and unrefined expression of traditional Filipino Tuba, made with only fresh coconut sap and naturally fermented for a refreshing and clean taste.
